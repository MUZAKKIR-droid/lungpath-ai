Common Questions

Answers to clinical and technical inquiries about the AI diagnostic system.

System Accuracy & Clinical Reliability

Our AI model utilizes ResNet-18 architecture trained on validated medical imaging datasets. While it achieves high sensitivity, it is designed for screening support and should not be used as a standalone diagnostic tool.

Data Privacy & Handling

Uploaded scans are processed in an isolated container environment. No patient identifiable information (PII) is persisted on our servers beyond the active analysis session.

Next Steps After Result

If the system identifies potential abnormalities, you should download the PDF report and consult a board-certified pulmonologist. You can view our directory of specialists on the specialists page.

Supported Imaging Modalities

The current model supports Chest Computed Tomography (CT) scans and standard posterior-anterior (PA) chest radiographs (X-Rays).
