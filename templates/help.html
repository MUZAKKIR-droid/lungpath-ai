How to use LungDetect AI

  1. Navigate to the Home Page.
  2. Click on the designated upload area or drag-and-drop your medical scan image (CT or X-Ray).
  3. Review the image preview to ensure you selected the correct file.
  4. Click the Analyze Image button.
  5. Wait a few seconds for our Deep Learning Model to process the scan.
  6. Review your results and AI confidence score on the Analysis Report page.
  7. If you wish to calculate your personal risk factors, visit the Risk Assessment page.
